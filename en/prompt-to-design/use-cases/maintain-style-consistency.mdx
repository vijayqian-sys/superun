---
title: "Maintain style consistency across teams"
---


## Scenario
Teams often design pages separately, leading to inconsistent colors, typography, and spacing across the product.

## Challenge
Ensuring alignment manually is time-consuming and error-prone.

## How prompt.to.design helps

- Open the plugin in Figma and select the **Style Transfer** feature  
- Choose a finalized design **Frame** as your reference  
- Enter your prompt, select device size, and click **Generate**  

<img 
  src="/images/maintain-style-consistency/1.png" 
  alt="Style Transfer setup"
  style={{ marginTop: '1rem', borderRadius: '8px' }}
/>


- Generation begins. The right panel will show the AI's analysis of the reference style and your prompt.

<img 
  src="/images/maintain-style-consistency/2.png" 
  alt="AI Analysis"
  style={{ marginTop: '1rem', borderRadius: '8px' }}
/>

- Review the result, refine the output, or **Import to Figma**.

  src="/images/maintain-style-consistency/3.png" 
  alt="Preview generated output"
  style={{ marginTop: '1rem', borderRadius: '8px' }}
