After each generation—whether it's **Kickstart**, **Style Transfer**, **Region Design**, or **Smart Fill**—Prompt.to.design displays a unified result panel.  
This page explains every part of the result view and how to use it.

---

## 🖼 Preview Panel

The **Preview Panel** shows your generated layout as a scrollable long image.

### What you can do:
- **Hover to zoom** and inspect details  
- **Scroll** to view the full generated layout  
- **Click sections** to jump to highlighted areas  

![Preview](../../../images/generation-result/preview.png)

---

## 🔍 Hover Zoom

Move your mouse over the preview to zoom in on any section.

This helps you:
- Inspect spacing  
- Check typography  
- Preview component clarity  

![Hover Zoom](../../../images/generation-result/hover.png)

---

## 🧠 Analysis & Summary

Switch to this tab to understand how the system interpreted your prompt.

You’ll see:
- **Analysis** — understanding of your intent  
- **Summary** — the structure & key sections generated  

![Analysis](../../../images/generation-result/analysis.png)

---

## 🕘 View History

The **History Panel** shows each generation step.

You can:
- Review previous prompts  
- Revisit earlier versions  
- Compare improvements  

![History](../../../images/generation-result/history.png)

---

## 🌐 Open Demo in superun

Click **Open demo in superun** to view the generated layout in a full webpage.

Why use it:
- See the design at real scale  
- Scroll smoothly  
- Preview in browser context  

![Demo](../../../images/generation-result/demo.png)

---

## ✏️ Refine Your Result

Use **Refine** to adjust the current version *without starting over*.

Examples:
- “Make spacing larger”
- “Switch to dark theme”
- “Update hero layout”

![Refine](../../../images/generation-result/refine.png)

---

## 🛠 Import to Figma

When you're satisfied, click **Import to Figma**.

This will:
- Bring the generated layout into your Figma file  
- Convert it into editable frames, Auto Layout, text layers, and components  
- Maintain styling consistency  

---

## 👍 / 👎 Provide Feedback

At the bottom-left, you can rate each generation.

- 👍 Good result  
- 👎 Didn’t match expectations  
- 💬 Add comments to improve future model performance  

---

## 🎯 Summary

This result panel is shared across all features.  
No matter how you generate your layout, you will always see:

- Preview  
- Analysis & Summary  
- Hover Zoom  
- History  
- Demo View  
- Refine  
- Import to Figma  
- Feedback tools  

This page gives you full control over every generation and helps you iterate quickly with confidence.
