superun makes it easy to publish your project and share it with teammates or users. You can iterate and update the live version anytime, and connect a custom domain.

> Before publishing, run a quick security check to avoid exposing sensitive data.

## How to publish

1. Navigate to the **Launch** section in your project
2. Select the **Publish** tab
3. Click the **Publish** button to start publishing your project
4. Wait for the publishing process to complete
5. Once published, you can share your project through multiple channels
