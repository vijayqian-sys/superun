## Scenario
You want to redesign just a hero section, card group, or footer.

## Challenge
Fixing one block often breaks the layout or requires manual adjustments.

## How prompt.to.design helps

- Select the region you want to improve  
- Describe what you want updated  
- AI rewrites only that specific region and keeps the rest of the layout untouched
