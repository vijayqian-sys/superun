## Scenario
You need a fast draft for a landing page, app screen, or dashboard.

## Challenge
Starting from an empty canvas can be slow and creativity-blocking.

## How prompt.to.design helps

- Describe what you want using natural language  
  Example: **“AI SaaS landing page with a hero section and pricing table”**  
- Choose your device size and click **Generate**  
- The plugin instantly produces a structured layout and visual direction
